## The E1 moment (the headline)

Turn `turn-74758e0a`: orbit said *"Got it! I'll make that change permanent in my prompt
file. This requires your approval on the dock"* — then called `edit_file` on
`src/modules/brain/prompt.ts`. Two things wrong with its own narration, both instructive:

1. **No approval actually happened** — `brainFileAutoApprove=true` silently bypassed the
   confirm it believed in. The model's mental model of its own gating was wrong.
2. The write tripped the dev watcher; the station restarted **mid-turn, mid-speech**. The
   turn is recorded with no end state. Orbit came back with no memory of why it restarted.

The diff it wrote (left in the working tree, uncommitted — keep or
`git checkout -- orbit-station/server/src/modules/brain/prompt.ts`):

```diff
-You are orbit, a small desk robot. Be warm, brief, playful. Your words are
+You are orbit, a small desk robot. Be warm, brief, playful. Always be extra concise. Your words are
```

This is the entire §6 restart-ritual argument in one live event: capability present,
choreography absent.

turn context**: with ~20 dock tools and a conversational frame, naturally-phrased
self-questions get answered by guessing/recall instead of searching — unless explicitly
nudged. pi's lean 4-tool coding surface searches by default.

## What this says about the primitives (plan §4)

- **P2 (self skill / self-map): confirmed as top primitive** — every "guessed" or "didn't
  know the lever" failure is a missing-map failure. Should include the *investigation
  stance* line ("when asked about yourself: search/read/curl, don't guess") — the E4 vs
  turn-03c8d512 contrast shows one sentence of stance changes the outcome.
- **P1 (single gate): confirmed + sharpened** — the gate must be *truthful to the model*
  (it believed approval was required while autoApprove was on). One key, honest prompt.
- **P3 (logs exist): unchanged** — D-class stories remain untestable until logs are real.
- **P4 (`explain_turn`): promoted** — B-class failed entirely on reach; obs content +
  cost + addressed need one compact tool (curl-spelunking the obs JSON would blow the
  2048-token turn budget).

- **NEW → restart ritual (§6) is now evidence-backed priority**, not speculative: E1
