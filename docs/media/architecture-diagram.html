orbit · architecture

One brain. Many bodies. A closed loop.

Every body — desk dock, floor rover, a wearable — streams what it senses into one shared perception layer, where swappable vision, audio, and language models turn raw signal into understanding. Agents reason over it, drawing on a persistent self — memory, ego, personality — that grows with every turn, and send actions back out to the bodies. The robot then senses its own actions, and the loop closes.
